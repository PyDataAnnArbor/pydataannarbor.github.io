Lightning Talks

written by Sean Law and Benjamin Zaitlen on 2019-07-10

Speakers sign-up here (https://pydataannarbor.github.io/lightning-talk-sign-up/)

Share your data story! From successes to failures and from beginner to advanced, we want to hear about your experience. Talks are expected to be 7 minutes or less, completely unpolished, and presented in rapid-fire succession. We'd love to hear your story!

Potential Topics:

Job advertising Data science Quick tutorial Demo an interesting project Academic research

Please review our code-of-conduct (https://pydata.org/code-of-conduct.html)
